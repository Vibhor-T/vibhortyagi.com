About

The Automotive Lens

I have been a die-hard car fanatic since birth. My conscious immersion into the industry began in the early 2000s—not as a career, but as a genuine obsession. Fueled by magazines, broadcasts, and forums, this engagement evolved from private passion to public contribution with the 2008 launch of theautomotiveindia.com. Over nearly two decades, my focus has shifted from the metal and specs to the human experience behind the wheel. I see cars not just as machines, but as a reflection of the people who drive them.

The Cognitive Foundation

Parallel to this, my life has been defined by what lies beneath the surface. My foundation is built on a synthesis of over 80 disciplines—spanning the spiritual, metaphysical, and universal, to the sciences of the mind and human behavior. This breadth of knowledge is sustained by a state of cosmic consciousness, offering a perspective that transcends limitations of ordinary observation.

Methodology & Worldview

Operating from this vantage point, I view the world through a lens of First Principles. Rather than relying on trends or generic advice, I prefer to strip complex situations down to their core components to understand how they actually function.

This synthesis of technical industry experience and a lifelong study of mental science allows me to approach problems with a perspective that is both grounded and expanded. Outside of these systems, my worldview is shaped by global travel, exploration of diverse cultures, and a steadfast commitment to wildlife conservation and prosocial initiatives. These interests are the foundation that keeps me connected to the broader picture.
